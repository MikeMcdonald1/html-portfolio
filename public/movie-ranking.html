The Best Movies According to Mike

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Goodfellas

I could watch this every day for the rest of my life.

Apocalypse Now

One of the deepest, darkest dives into human nature. Like the main character, you also begin to question your sanity as the movie drifts along.

Dumb & Dumber

This movie brings nothing but joy for me every single time!
